Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (email, password)
  • Profile information (name, photos, bio, preferences)
  • Faith and religious preferences
  • Messages and communications with other users
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our matchmaking service
  • Connect you with compatible matches
  • Send you notifications about matches and messages
  • Ensure the safety and security of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Other users (as part of your public profile)
  • Service providers who help us operate the platform
  • Law enforcement when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account at any time.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at privacy@douainimi.com.
